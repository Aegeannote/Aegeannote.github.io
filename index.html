A note of flavour from the Aegean

Aegean Note is a culinary concept that brings the essence of Greece to London — through fresh, seasonal ingredients and intimate, home-based dining experiences. We specialise in wild-caught fish flown in weekly from trusted Greek fishermen, alongside a carefully selected range of premium seasonal products from across the islands and mainland. Our focus is on quality, simplicity, and provenance. Our private chef service transforms your home into a Greek table. With bespoke menus built around the best ingredients of the moment, we create memorable dining experiences that taste like summer on the Aegean — no passport required!

Why Choose us

At Aegean Note, we provide a truly authentic Greek culinary experience that stands apart. Our commitment to sourcing the freshest, most seasonal seafood directly from Greece ensures that you receive ingredients of unparalleled quality. With deep expertise in Greek seafood, we bring the essence of the Cycladic Tavern to your table, offering flavors and experiences that are both rare and memorable.

Sharing the soul of the Cyclades

At Aegean Note, our vision extends beyond merely selling fine fresh wild fish sourced from Greece. We are dedicated to highlighting the numerous health benefits of consuming fish regularly. Through our services, we aim to educate and inspire individuals to incorporate fish into their diets, promoting overall well-being and vitality.

How It Works

1

Chat with us

Send us a message or call to tell us about your event - number of guests, dietary needs, preferences, and the occasion. We recommend reaching out at least 7 days in advance.
2

Menu Curation

We order the freshest seasonal products directly from Greece - from wild fish to specialty ingredients - and begin crafting a bespoke menu just for you.

Our chef arrives at your home with everything needed. Each dish is prepared on-site and served live, with insight into the ingredients and stories behind every plate.
4

When dinner ends, we quietly reset your kitchen - leaving it cleaner than we found it, so you can savour the experience, not the cleanup.

About

the Founder

How it all started..

What began as a personal obsession - sourcing the freshest catch from Greece - has become a mission to share that quality and warmth with others, right here in London.

My journey began several years ago during my pursuit of a master's degree at a distinguished university in London. It was during this time that I discerned a notable absence of freshly sourced Greek wild fish, even within the most affluent and opulent neighborhoods. Motivated by this discerned gap in the market, I felt compelled to embark on a mission to address this deficiency and provide discerning businesses, individuals and families with access to the finest quality seafood.
